Plotting simple 3D graph with Mayavi
====================================

A simple example of the plot of a 3D graph with Mayavi
in order to test the autonomy of the gallery.



.. code-block:: python

    mlab.test_plot3d()

    mlab.figure()
    mlab.test_contour3d()
